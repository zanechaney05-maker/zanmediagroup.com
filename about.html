Why Zan Media Group exists

Most local service businesses are great at the work and stretched thin on everything else. Marketing usually means word of mouth, a few yard signs, and maybe a boosted post that went nowhere. Meanwhile, the homeowners who need a roofer, an HVAC tech, or a plumber this week are scrolling Facebook and Instagram every day.

Zan Media Group was started in Wilmington, NC by founder Zane Chaney to close that gap: run the ads properly, track every dollar, and hand business owners what they actually want, which is a phone that rings with real jobs.

How we work

We're a founder-run agency, and that's deliberate. Your account isn't handed off to a junior media buyer juggling forty clients. The person who pitched you is the person building your campaigns, watching your numbers, and answering your texts.

We're Meta Blueprint trained and we specialize in one thing: lead generation on Facebook and Instagram for local service businesses. No SEO retainers, no logo packages, no social media management fluff. Depth over breadth.

What we believe

Transparency beats hype. Your ad spend goes straight to Meta from your own account, never marked up. You see the same numbers we see.

Results are the only deliverable. Impressions and reach don't pay your crew. Booked jobs do, so that's what we optimize for and that's what we report on.

Local matters. We're based in Wilmington and we know the Cape Fear market: the neighborhoods, the seasonality, what homeowners here respond to. That context shows up in better-performing ads.
