Information we collect

When you submit our contact form, we collect the information you provide: your name, business name, phone number, and email address. We also collect standard website usage information through cookies and similar technologies, such as the pages you visit and how you arrived at our site.

How we use your information

We use your contact information to respond to your inquiry, discuss our services with you, and follow up about working together. We use website usage data to understand how visitors use our site and to measure and improve our advertising.

Meta Pixel and advertising

This website uses the Meta Pixel, a tool provided by Meta Platforms, Inc., to measure the effectiveness of our advertising on Facebook and Instagram. The Pixel collects information such as pages visited and actions taken on our site. When you submit our contact form, we may share a hashed (cryptographically scrambled) version of your contact details with Meta to help match conversions to our ads. Meta cannot read this hashed information directly; it is used only for matching purposes. You can learn more about how Meta handles this data in Meta's Privacy Policy, and you can adjust your ad preferences in your Facebook settings.

Cookies

We use first-party and third-party cookies to operate the site and support our advertising measurement as described above. You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies will not prevent you from using this site.

Third-party services
